# Strategic Decision Log

Major direction decisions and their rationale. When you question a decision months later, read why you made it instead of reconstructing from memory.

**When to log:** Career moves, location choices, major financial commitments, relationship decisions, strategic pivots — anything where future-you might ask "why did I choose this?"

**Not for:** Tactical decisions (which hotel, which tool, which route). Those belong in project files.
