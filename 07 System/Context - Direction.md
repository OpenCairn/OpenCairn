## Maintenance

- **Values:** Reviewed weekly during `/weekly-review` — pull out what needs emphasis this week
- **Strategic plans:** Overhauled at major life transitions (new job, new city, new chapter). Tweakable anytime. At minimum, reviewed during `/quarterly-review`
- **Anti-goals:** Append-only. Add when you make a deliberate "no" decision. Remove only if premises genuinely changed
- **Disciplines:** Evolving. Add/remove as life changes. Review during `/quarterly-review`
